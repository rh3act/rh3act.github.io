Contradictions of perspective. Contrasts of light. Contrasts of form. Points of view impossible to achieve in drawing and painting. Foreshortenings with a strong distortion of the objects, with a crude handling of matter. Moments altogether new, never seen before compositions whose boldness outstrips the imagination of painters Then the creation of those instants which do not exist, contrived by means of photomontage. The negative transmits altogether new stimuli to the sentient mind and eye.

RIZLA EDITIONS

The RIZLA editions is a graphic design project which reinmagines the covers of the famous rolling paper brand. It was inspired by music generes as well as times of the year.

PHOTOCOPIED THOUGHTS

Ever wondered where your thoughts come from? If someone somewhere said it already? Well of course, and it seems that in this technological era even more thoughts are as widespread as the infamous one "Cogito, ergo sum." But if you really "Are by thinking" than you are not yourself if your thoughts are generated by another person. This series is a little experiment with digital collage an typography which represent thoughts that remained rooted deep inside of me when heard.

RINOMA EP

Rinoma is a young artist an producer from a small town in Croatia who makes jazzy trip-hop beats. It was this kind of music inspired the booklet and the CD covers. It was imagined that an alien species desended to Earth and seeing rather many cathastophies acted less evolved than the human species to avoid having to deal with the “superior beings”.
